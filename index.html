Status of Long-Term Drinking Water Advisories on Public Systems on Reserves

The Government of Canada is working with First Nations to end long-term drinking water advisories which have been in effect for more than 12 months since November 2015. Indigenous Services Canada maintains a map of these advisories. The data behind this map has been re-purposed to create the charts below.
